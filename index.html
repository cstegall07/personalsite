CHARMAINE

Explore this page to learn everything about Char.

Origin

Char was born in Atlanta, Ga and raised in College Park, Ga. She is the seconnd oldest of three siblings: Tremayne, Alexis, and Antonio. Charmaine went to Banneker High School graduating in the top ten percent of her class then attending Howard University. While at Howard, she studied psychology and graphic design. She was Creative Director for a modeling organization called Models of the Mecca, where she created photoshoot concepts and directed over 60+ models and crew. Char also was technical producer of a podcast and a mentor during her attendance of Howard.

Aspirations

At a young age, Char had dreams of becoming a fashion designer and a surgeon. She went to Howard University to study psychology and eventually realized that would not fulfill her either. She is now studying to become a Front End Developer and UX Designer. So far she is enjoying her journey and creating websites. (This is one of those websites).
